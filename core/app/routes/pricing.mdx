## Frequently Asked Questions

### What's the difference between OSS and SaaS versions?

The OSS version is free and self-hosted with unlimited plugins. SaaS versions (Basic, Team, Enterprise) are cloud-hosted with advanced plugins, AI features, and professional support.

### Is there a free trial?

Yes, Basic and Team plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required.

### Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades are applied at the next billing cycle.

### Can I export my data?

Yes, all plans support data export in JSON and CSV formats.

### Can I develop custom plugins?

Custom plugins are supported in OSS and Enterprise plans. Check our plugin development guide for details.
